Welcome to Abode

Abode is a venture capital studio backing bold founders who are reshaping the future. We partner from the earliest stages, helping startups unlock real-world impact through capital, connections, and operational support.

Whether it’s AI, fintech, deeptech or climate innovation — we seek teams building with ambition, clarity, and grit. Abode isn’t just capital. We’re your first believer, your sounding board, and your launchpad.

Ready to Start With Abode?

We're here to answer your questions and guide your journey from idea to scale.

What stage does Abode typically invest in?

We primarily invest at the pre-seed and seed stages, working closely with founders from day zero to build scalable, venture-backable companies.

What sectors does Abode focus on?

We back startups across AI, fintech, SaaS, and frontier technologies that drive global impact.

Do you provide strategic or operational support?

Yes. We provide hands-on support with hiring, GTM strategy, fundraising, and more — not just capital.

How can I pitch my startup to Abode?

You can fill out the form or email us directly with a brief intro deck. We review every submission and respond within 7 days.
